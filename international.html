WashU for International Students

Why an American Education?

Coming to the United States from overseas is a life-changing experience. It can be expensive and daunting - but it's worth it. You'll get a world-class education at WashU alongside both native American students and fellow international students. We have all the resources you need to be successful, happy, and productive as a student and individual. You'll learn critical thinking skills, hone your interpersonal ideas, and be exposed to a plethora of cultures other than your own. Keep reading for more information!

International Departments

Our international student program has been around for decades. As a result, we have tons of infrastructure in place designed to make your experience as seamless and enjoyable as possible. A great example of this is the variety of departments tailored specifically towards overseas students, such as:

  • Advanced Studies in Chinese
  • German Topics
  • Italian Research

You won't be limited to the above areas of study. At WashU, it's always possible to custom-tailor your experience so that you're studying exactly what you want to be all the time.

International Resources

There exists a multitude of resources for international students in addition to our tried-and-tested academic-specific programs. At WashU, we make the following services readily available to anybody who needs them:

  • Office for International Students and Scholars
  • Full Orientation Programs
  • Visa Application Help
  • Community Connection/Immersion Programs
