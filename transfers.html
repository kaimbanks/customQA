WashU for Transfers

Requirements

Because WashU is such a renowned academic institituion, it stands to reason that we have rigorous transfer requirements. As much as we'd love to admit everybody with a thirst for education, our small student body size inhibits us from doing so. Transfer students must meet the following requirements in order to be eligible:

  • Cumulative GPA of 3.5 or higher
  • Official High School Diploma
  • At least one completed semester of studies at a recognized college-level academic institution
  • Official transcript detailing at least some university-level coursework

Financial Aid Options

Here at WashU, we pride ourselves on having one of the best financial aid offices in the country. Please feel free to reach out to us by phone (888-547-6670) oremailus anytime. Aid packages range from simple supplies grants to full academic scholarships, as long as a need for such assistance is demonstrated.

Why WashU?

We realize that every student looking at this page is also likely looking at many others just like it presented by other universities. So why should you choose WashU? Take a look at the following list to find out:

  • We have some of the most renowned departments in the country.
  • Your experience is tailored around you. You have near-complete freedom in course selection, and switching majors is as easy as you can imagine.
  • Your happiness and mental health are paramount. As such, we make every effort to ensure that all your needs are met, from housing to food choices to social opportunities.
